Help Page

Welcome to VitalWatch! VitalWatch is your companion to gamify managing your lifestyle and make daily tasks more fun and engaging.

How to Use VitalWatch:

  1. Complete Your Daily Tasks: Check off items on your personalized daily to-do list.
  2. Earn Points: Each completed task rewards you with points.
  3. Spin the Prize Wheel: Use your points to spin the prize wheel and unlock exciting rewards.

We want to make leading a healthy lifestyle more accessible, so we’ve included plenty of resources throughout the app—featuring everything from places to eat, parks, bike trails, museums, and local events.
